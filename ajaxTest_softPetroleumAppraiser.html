Featured software product: softPetroleum Appraiser™

softPetroleum Appraiser™ is a full featured petroleum appraisal software system used in petroleum asset valuations and reserves determination. Unlike most other petroleum appraisal software, softPetroleum Appraiser™ has been designed to also perform specialized valuation functions used in the property tax / minerals tax area. As an example of this built-in specialization, the following e-brochure highlights the special features applicable to the property tax valuation practices in the state of Kansas. softPetroleum Appraiser™ also has property tax valuation specialization built-in for other major oil and gas states.
